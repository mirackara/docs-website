Synthetic monitoring can help you proactively catch and resolve issues in your page, web apps, or API endpoints before they affect customer workflow.

## Get started with simple browser monitor [#getstarted]

<Steps>

<Step>
## Sign into your New Relic account [#signin]

You don't need an installation to get started with synthetics. Sign in to your New Relic account, go to **[one.newrelic.com/synthetics-nerdlets](https://one.newrelic.com/synthetics-nerdlets)**, then click **Create a monitor**. 
</Step>

<Step>
## Paste in a  URL [#url]

Testing page load availability and functionality only requires a URL. Paste it into the field, then let some time pass for your new monitor to report data.

<Video
    type="wistia"
    id="3cccmbp3o8"
/>

</Step>

<Step>
## View your data [#data]

When your monitor makes its first report, you can view your data in the New Relic UI.

</figcaption>

</Step>

</Steps>
